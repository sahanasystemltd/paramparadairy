About Us

A & M is one of the leading manufacturer of Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (Jumbo Bags), Four Loop Bags, Corner Loop Bags, UN Bags, Baffle bags & Sift Proof Bags. Apart from this the company has started business of dealing in Milk Trading, Processing, and supply chain.
We are located in Gota, Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India.

Advantages of FIBC

We are one of the prestigious manufacturer and supplier of Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers (FIBC) that are designed as per the need of various several small and large scale industries in India. FIBC Bags are more demanding in indian and international markets. We offer these Bulk Bags in various specifications.

  • FIBCs are easy to handle for Transport & Warehousing purpose.
  • FIBCs are easy to transport. With a wide variety of Lifting Oprions available these bags can be transporteted in a very easy and simple way.
  • FIBCs are available in several standards and specifications and are renowned for long lasting & its durablality.
  • FIBCs are the easiest way to transport and move bulk materials used for Exports.
